3D Modeling

Since purchasing my first 3D printer I have spent countless hours designing practical prints, repair parts and modifications. With over 8 years of working with CAD for additive manufacturing I've become quite confident in my ability to design nearly anything you could 3D print. Here I will link my Printable profile which now contains all my currently posted designs. As well as my Etsy shop which I started this year to sell Custom TPU Analogue Pocket Cases. I also design my own FPV parts so feel free to reach out with any print or design request!
