FPV

Not quite sure why it took me so long to find FPV since it has been nothing but a dream, but it is a new excitement in my life as of 2022. After helping a co-worker at the dive shop fix up a few of his drones I was hooked. I've since gotten my Basic Operations drone Pilot Certification and registered my personal drones with Transport Canada. This summer I hope to complete the testing needed to get my Advanced Operations Certificate so that I can begin flying for commercial jobs and in close courters to people.

Until then I'll share the first edited video I made for Hal-con's Closing Ceremonies in 2022 and a link to the Mighty Oak FPV YouTube channel where I have began to post lightly edited videos from some of my flights.
